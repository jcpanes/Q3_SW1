My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic




Introduction

Once upon a time, in the land of Equestria, Princess Celestia assigns her most faithful student, Twilight Sparkle, her most important lesson. To learn about the Magic of Friendship! In order to do so, Twilight Sparkle moves to a small town named Ponyville. There she meets five very special ponies: Applejack, Rarity, Fluttershy, Rainbow Dash, and Pinkie Pie. Together they form the Elements of Harmony, and this is how they discover that Friendship is Magic.





Characters


Twilight Sparkle

Bookish and intelligent, Twilight represents the element of Magic. She has a baby dragon named Spike as her most helpful assistant. In fact, she got accepted into Princess Celestia's Magic School for Gifted Unicorns because she was able to get Spike to hatch from his egg.


Applejack

Applejack is the most hardworking Earth pony around. She lives and works with her family on Sweet Apple Acres. Where they make the most delicious apple treats. Applejack represents the element of Honesty.

Rarity

Fashion! Is the passion of fair Rarity. She owns a boutique called Carousel Boutique. A place where you will find the most captivating designs you will ever see. Although, she never hesitates to share her gems with anyone who needs them. Rarity represents the element of Generosity.


Fluttershy

Though squeamish around other ponies, Fluttershy has a natural affinity toward animals. She makes sure to take care of all the little creatures. She might be a little shy, but she has the biggest heart of all. Fluttershy represents the element of Kindness.

Rainbow Dash

Becoming a Wonderbolt is one of Rainbow Dash's greatest dreams. She's probably the fastest pegasi around, so it's not hard to imagine. She's the first and only pony ever to create a Sonic Rainboom. Rainbow Dash represents the element of Loyalty.


Pinkie Pie

If there's a party, it's safe to bet that Pinkie Pie is around. She throws the most amazing parties ever! She loves making everyone happy with her quirky jokes and hyperactiveness. Pinkie Pie represents the element of Laughter.
